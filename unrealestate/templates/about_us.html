{% trans "Expanding on real estate investments" %}

{% blocktrans %} Aftermarket real-estate may offer great investment opportunity, but comes with hassles for buyers and sellers. Real estate is an emotional, engaging and tangible asset class, but it is presented and sold in uninspiring ways. How might real estate investors get a better chance to connect with the investments they make, and know what they are putting their money into without much manual due diligence? {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Taking the experience economy to experiential asset classes like real estate is a no brainer. It is time we break down big financial decisions into smaller, digestible chunks that allow us to better diversify our assets and enjoy investing by exploring the tangible side of where we put our money. It is ironic how much we care about experience in the small things we purchase everyday, yet how willingly we accept a subpar experience whenever large sums of money are involved? Turning big financial decisions such as investing in real estate into small and more exciting chunks that come more naturally to us is a tremendous and little-exploited opportunity. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Drawing inspiration from crowdfunding, virtual reality technology and the inherent preference in Asia for real estate investment, unrealestate offers a delightful and intuitive avenue to grow one's wealth by backing attractive aftermarket real estate sales harnessing the power of group buying. This empowers anyone to own a piece of desirable real estate, be it a holiday home or a high-end condomimium downtown and earn rental income on it immediately. Also, investors have the opportunity to take advantage of the healthy returns on real estate commonly seen in Asia. Virtual walkthroughs facilitate property evaluation and build an emotional connection between asset and the group of buyers. Crowd based decision-making and an aftermarket for ionvestors to sell their stakes to oher users help make investments in property highly tradable, with the additional intangible benefit of owning a high-status, lifestyle asset class. {% endblocktrans %}
